Frequently Asked Questions

Can I listen with my screen off?

Unfortunately, no. Due to restrictions from mobile operating systems (iOS/Android) and YouTube's policies, audio cannot play from a browser when the screen is off. You can, however, use the Picture-in-Picture (PiP) button to keep the video playing in a small window while you use other apps or browse other sites.

Is this service really free?

Yes, absolutely. This is a demonstration app and is 100% free to use. All features are available without any cost or subscription.

Where are my favorites stored?

Your favorite videos are stored in your browser's `localStorage`. This means they are saved on your device and will be there when you return, but they won't sync across different computers or browsers.
